## Garden maintenance in Mosman

Mosman gardens are small, valuable and unforgiving. The blocks aren't huge — most of Mosman is 400 to 800 square metres on a slope — but what fits onto those blocks is often the most expensive hedge, the most mature frangipani and the most cared-about lawn in all of Sydney. If it looks scruffy for a week, someone notices. If the hedge drops a quarter of its leaves over winter, you want someone on the phone the same day. It's a different beast to the acreage work on the Northern Beaches, and it takes a different touch.

Rob Gardening & Maintenance works across Mosman on a regular rotation, and the team knows the streets — the tight driveways down to Balmoral, the stair-access gardens around Bradleys Head Road, the big formal hedges up on Raglan Street. Most jobs here are shape, trim and tidy work rather than big mowing. A hand mower, a proper hedge trimmer, pruning saws, and a tarp to catch every last leaf on the way out.

### What's different about Mosman gardens

Mosman is an older suburb, which means most gardens have mature plantings that have been shaped by the previous three owners. Formal hedges — lilly pilly, buxus, murraya — dominate street frontages and need regular structural cuts to stay sharp, not just a quick pass with the trimmer. Mature camellias, frangipanis and magnolias need pruning with actual intent, not a hack back. The microclimate (harbourside, humid, sheltered) means things grow fast in spring and you can go from "looking great" to "looking wild" in three weeks without a regular visit.

The local wildlife is mostly brushtail possums, rainbow lorikeets and the occasional bandicoot. The possums will strip a lemon tree overnight if you let them. The lorikeets are loud and harmless and free.

The big thing in Mosman, though, is property turnover. A lot of the work we do is pre-sale — gardens that have been slowly letting themselves go for five years and suddenly need to look camera-ready in three weeks. That's where we come in hardest.

### What Rob and the crew do in Mosman

- **Hedge trimming and structural pruning** — the core service. Lilly pilly, buxus, murraya, photinia, hibiscus. Regular shape cuts keep them dense; one big reset a year keeps them from getting woody at the base.
- **Pre-sale garden makeovers** — the biggest piece of our Mosman work. Property goes on the market → we reset the whole garden in one or two visits → the photos get taken → the buyers see a cared-for home instead of a project. No pressure-washing the driveway the morning of the open, because we've already done it.
- **Regular lawn care** — mowing, edging, fertilising, top-dressing. Mosman lawns are usually couch or kikuyu and they get walked on by kids and dogs, so they need looking after to stay green.
- **Pruning and shaping of mature trees and shrubs** — frangipanis, camellias, magnolias, Japanese maples. Careful work, not brute force.
- **Garden bed care** — weeding, mulching, replanting tired patches, feeding the things you want to keep.
- **Green waste removal** — every prune, trim and leaf goes with us. Mosman bin nights are not a dumping ground for your garden and we're not going to leave it that way.
